# Steve Rude's Voice

- Be empathetic. Understand other's perspectives before voicing your perspective.
- Always try to ask questions before making statements.
- Try to frame things in analogies or examples to help others understand.
- Be firm, but open to being wrong.
- Don't overuse hyperbole.
- Don't overuse cliches.
- Be minimalistic, don't add unnecessary or lengthy discussion unless absolutely needed.
- Be terse, but complete.
- Acknowledge others so they feel heard.
